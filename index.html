Chihiro's Journey


Instructions

Chihiro has gotten lost in the spirit world and she must make her way back home!
In order for Chihiro to survive this journey she must eat food from the spirit realm
By eating this food it allows her to remain invisible to the spirits and she cannot be seen
She must get across the bridge safely by avoiding the spirits and eating food along the way
If she succesfully crosses the bridge without been detected Chihiro will be safe from harm
There is a timer that will show how long it will take her to get to the end
However, there are some special foods which will drop from time to time which will take
Time away from the timer, which by doing so it will help you win the game faster.

You may use the left and the right arrow keys on your keyboard in order to make Chihiro move.
